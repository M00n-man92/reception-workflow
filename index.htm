System Overview

The Visitor Management System enables reception teams to efficiently manage visitor arrivals, appointments, approvals, and departures from a centralized dashboard. The application streamlines the visitor experience while ensuring hosts are informed and involved in the approval process.

Receptionists can schedule appointments in advance or register walk-in visitors, capture visitor details, optionally collect identification and signatures, and initiate the check-in process. Once a visitor has been registered, the assigned host receives a Microsoft Teams notification containing the visitor's information and can approve or reject the visit directly from Teams.

The system tracks each visitor throughout their entire lifecycle, from appointment scheduling through check-in, approval, and checkout. Real-time visitor status updates allow reception staff to quickly identify visitors currently on-site, pending approval requests, completed visits, and cancelled appointments.

Core Capabilities

  • Secure authentication using Email/Password or Microsoft 365 accounts
  • Reception desk configuration and floor assignment management
  • Visitor appointment scheduling and administration
  • Walk-in visitor registration and check-in workflows
  • Optional visitor ID capture and digital signature collection
  • Host approval and rejection workflows through Microsoft Teams
  • Real-time visitor status tracking and dashboard monitoring
  • Visitor checkout and visit completion management

Visitor Lifecycle

Visitors typically progress through the following statuses: Scheduled → Pending → Checked In → Checked Out. Visitors may also transition to a Cancelled status when a host or receptionist rejects the visit request.
